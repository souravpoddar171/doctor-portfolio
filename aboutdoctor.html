About Dr. Ashwini Gaurav

Dr. Ashwini Gaurav is the Best Orthopaedic Doctor in Patna. He is a famous Joint Replacement Surgeon of Bihar. He has done MBBS (Hons) from Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College, Bhagalpur. He is very high ranked in All India Post Graduate Examinations and he chose to do his masters from Patna. He did his MS (Orthopedics) from the famous Patna Medical College. He received the “Gold Medal” for his exceptional performance in the MS examinations.

Thereafter Dr. Ashwini was selected as a senior resident at AIIMS, New Delhi. He worked and learned the management of complex fractures and Pelvic & Acetabular fractures at JPN Apex Trauma Centre, AIIMS, and New Delhi. Later, he came back to Patna to join as a Senior Resident at PMCH, Patna. After completing his residency, Dr. Ashwini Gaurav, went to Seoul, South Korea. Dr. Ashwin has worked At Seonam Hospital, Seoul, South Korea and learned from the master of Joint Replacement Surgery, Dr. Young Hoon Kim the intricacies of Complex Arthroplasty Surgery. He was awarded a fellowship from Ewah University, Seoul.

To pursue his interest in joint replacement surgeries, he went to Switzerland to work with the Hip team at the biggest hospital in the country. There at Inselspital, he learned the newer techniques of “Hip Preservation Surgery”, an upcoming domain in India. He was also awarded a Fellowship by Dr. Prof. Siebenrock after the completion and returned to India. He is an executive body member of the Bihar Orthopedic Association and also a life member of the Indian Orthopedic Association.

Dr. Ashwini Gaurav is a very academic doctor with more than 10 articles published in national and international journals. He is also the editorial member in “Journal of Orthopedic Diseases and Traumatology”. He is now working as a private consultant in Patna. He operates at major hospitals in the city and affordable charges. He is among the top 10 Joint Replacement Orthopedic surgeons of Bihar and a famous Pelvic-Acetabular surgeon.

Dr. Ashwini Gaurav treats all kinds of Joint Disorders / Arthritis including “Osteoarthritis”, “Rheumatoid Arthritis”, “Juvenile Rheumatoid Arthritis”, “Ankylosing Spondylitis” & “Avascular Necrosis” to name a few. He is always involved in the treatment of his patients and gives enough time to them during consultations. He has a special interest in Arthroplasty, Arthroscopy, and sports medicine. That’s why most of the people has say he is the best arthroscopy surgeon in Patna . He has been trained in prestigious hospitals in Europe and India.
